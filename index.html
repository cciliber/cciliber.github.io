CARLO CILIBERTO

Researcher in Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Robotics

About Me

Hi! My name is Carlo Ciliberto. I am a researcher in machine learning at the Laboratory for Computational and Statistical Learning, a joint lab between the Massachusetts Instiute of Technology (MIT) and the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT).

I am interested in theoretical aspects of machine learning as well as applications to real-world problems. I am particularly fashinated by problems involving structured data (both in input and/or output). See here current research interests in detail.
